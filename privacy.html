Privacy Policy

Subtetris — Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Subtetris is a single-player puzzle game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what information we collect and how we use it.

Data Collection

Subtetris does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically, the app does not:

  • Require account registration or login
  • Collect your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Access your location, camera, microphone, or contacts
  • Use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs
  • Transmit any data to external servers

Local Storage

Game state (such as your current score) may be stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

Subtetris does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, the app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
