Subtetris Support

A Tetris variant where blocks carry numbers and rows are cleared by subtraction.

How to Play

  • Stack falling pieces to fill rows
  • When a row is full, the smallest number in that row is subtracted from all blocks
  • Blocks reduced to zero vanish — the stack above falls down
  • Chain reactions and multi-row clears earn bonus points
  • Tap NEW GAME to start, PAUSE to pause
  • Use NUMS to cycle block number range (1–2, 1–3, 1–4)

Controls

  • ← → — move piece left / right
  • ↺ — rotate piece
  • ▼ — soft drop (speed up fall)
  • Swipe on the board for quick gestures

Frequently Asked Questions

The game seems frozen after a row clears.
That's intentional — the subtraction and cascade animations play in slow motion so you can see what happened. The game resumes automatically.
What does the NUMS button do?
It cycles the maximum block number between 1–2, 1–3, and 1–4. Higher numbers make rows harder to clear but earn more points.
How is the score calculated?
Score is based on the number of rows cleared, the level, and the subtraction value. Multi-row clears and larger subtractions earn exponentially more points.
