Subtetris — Changelog
=====================

v1.5.103 (2026-04-08)
  - Falling rules rewritten per CLAUDE.md spec:
    · Rows processed one at a time (topmost first), not all simultaneously
    · Removed minNum-1 and fullRows-1 extra fall bonuses
    · New findHangingBlocks(): BFS from bottom row finds all blocks not connected to ground via 4-directional adjacency
    · New applyGravityUntilStable(): drops all hanging blocks 1 row, repeats until none remain
    · Score formula updated: 100 × level × minNum^1.5 × chainMult per row
